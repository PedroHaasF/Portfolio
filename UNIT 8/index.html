Minecraft Dungeons

Minecraft Dungeons was a dungeon crawler video game developed by Mojang Stockholm, Mojang Shanghai and Double Eleven and published by Xbox Game Studios. It is based on Minecraft and was released on May 26, 2020 for Microsoft Windows, Nintendo Switch, PlayStation 4, and Xbox One. It was discontinued on September 28, 2023.

Minecraft Legends

Minecraft Legends is a real-time strategy and action game. It has strategy elements at its core, but its mechanics are inspired by the "immersion of action games". It takes place in the Minecraft Overworld which is described as a "familiar but in many ways new overworld"
